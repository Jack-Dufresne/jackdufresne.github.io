About

I design, validate, and deploy mechanical and thermal infrastructure for large-scale compute environments.

Most of my work is L11 and L12 architecture for direct liquid cooling systems, including manifolds, CDUs, rear-door heat exchangers, racks, and supporting fluid networks.

I stay close to deployment realities: reliability, serviceability, contamination control, leak response, and instrumentation.
