About Me

Passionate about pushing the boundaries of compute infrastructure

I'm a Mechanical Engineer with a deep passion for designing and deploying cutting-edge compute infrastructure. My work centers on creating robust, efficient systems that power the next generation of AI and high-performance computing.

I specialize in direct liquid cooling systems, working across the entire stack from component-level design to full system integration. My expertise spans thermal management, mechanical architecture, and system validation.

Whether it's designing manifolds for optimal flow distribution, optimizing CDU configurations, or architecting rack-level solutions, I bring a systematic approach to solving complex engineering challenges.
